SEAXES

  • Cameron Daigle: Guitar & Vocals
  • Cooper Glodoski: Drums
  • Ron Petzke: Bass

Seaxes (pron. "Saxes") plays urgent, anxious, crashing noise rock with emotional vocals & impeccable sound. The trio of Cam Daigle (Teeth Kids), Cooper Glodoski (Teeth Kids) and Ron Petzke (Bongripper) form an overwhelming wall of auditory anxiety, with occasional earned release. Music for today.

Our Debut LP is coming this summer on The Ghost Is Clear Records.
